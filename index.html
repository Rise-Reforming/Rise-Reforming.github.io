Today's Waste, Tomorrow's Value

Today’s Waste has Tomorrow’s Value

At Rise Reforming, we seek to turn the tide on the plastic disaster using a revolutionary path to carbon-negative chemicals.

We see plastic waste as a virtually unlimited resource and envision a first of its kind plastic-to-dimethyl ether (DME) synthesis process. DME can be blended with LPG to make a drop-in alternative fuel used for domestic cooking/heating and transportation. Our blend improves LPG's cold-start performance while reducing its lifecycle emissions by 35%. DME is also an environmentally friendly propellant used in air fresheners, hair spray, spray paint, and insecticides.

We thus aim to make carbon negative DME (cnDME) at a price competitive with fossil-derived DME – all while erasing plastic waste.
