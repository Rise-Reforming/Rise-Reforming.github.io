a tale of two problems

Plastic Waste

Annually, 85% of plastic waste ends up in landfills where 40 percent decomposes in 25 years, but the other 60 percent stays around for thousands.

Fossil Based Chemicals

Fossil-based chemicals account for 5 percent of greenhouse gas emissions, and must be decarbonized by 2050 to meet U.N. net zero goals.

what if one solution could address both these issues?

Our novel chemical process converts plastic waste to carbon negative fuel in the form of dimethyl ether (DME), thus eliminating waste and providing clean energy simultaneously.
